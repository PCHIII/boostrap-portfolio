About Me

Hello, I’m Trey, a technology enthusiast with a Bachelor of Science in Management from the Georgia Institute of Technology. My educational background has been invaluable in my diverse professional endeavors.

Recently, I enhanced my technical skills by completing the Georgia Tech MERN Stack Web Development Program, gaining a deeper understanding of modern software development technologies. This training has sharpened my technical proficiency, enabling me to excel in Technical Sales, Sales Support and Solutions Engineering for Web Services, SaaS, and Software Application companies.

Throughout my career, I have held roles in Management, Business Development, and Sales, working with both small and large companies. Additionally, I successfully owned and operated my own business for about 10 years. My experience spans various industries, including Medical Devices and Healthcare Technologies, Energy, Agricultural Sciences, SaaS, and IT services.

On a personal note, I enjoy the outdoors and playing golf whenever I can. I live in Cumming, Ga with my wife and daughter.
