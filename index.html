About Me

Hello, I’m Trey, a technology enthusiast with a Bachelor of Science in Management from the Georgia Institute of Technology. My educational background has been invaluable in my diverse professional endeavors.

Over the past six years, I have served as a Solutions Engineer in two distinct roles: first for an e-commerce platform, and then for a converged identity platform that includes Identity Governance and Administration, Privileged Access Management, Application Access Governance, and Third-Party Access Governance.

In these roles, I bridged the gap between technical expertise and sales strategy by thoroughly understanding client needs, effectively communicating the platform’s value, and providing robust support throughout the sales process. I focused on emphasizing the most impactful features that directly addressed customer interests, highlighting usability, and showcasing the overall user experience. This approach was crucial in driving successful sales outcomes and ensuring high levels of client satisfaction.

Prior to that, I honed my technical skills by completing the Georgia Tech MERN Stack Web Development Program, where I gained a comprehensive understanding of modern software development technologies. This training significantly enhanced my technical proficiency, equipping me to excel in technical sales, sales support, and solutions engineering for web services, SaaS, and software application companies.

On a personal note, I enjoy the outdoors and playing golf whenever I can. I live in Cumming, Ga with my wife and daughter.

You can view the applications I’ve built on the Portfolio page. Additionally, check out the customized Okta Authentication Widget on the Login Tab for Multi-factor Authentication, linked to my Okta Developer Organization for Identity Access Management.

Thanks for visiting.
