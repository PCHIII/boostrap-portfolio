About Me

My name is Trey and I love all things technology. I have a Bachelor of Science from Georgia Institute of Technology in Management. This background has been extremely useful in just about everything I have been involved in.

I recently fine-tuned my skill set by completing the Ga Tech MERN Stack Bootcamp. This allowed me to gain a deeper understanding of the technology stack used to develop today's software platforms. My goal here was to increase my technical proficiency and provide better Technical Sales, Sales Support and Account Management for Web Services, SaaS and Software Application companies.

Historically, I have mostly been working in Management, Business Development and Sales. I have worked for both small and large companies and I also owned and operated my own company for about 10 years.

My experiences have touched on many different fields and industries from Medical Devices and Healthcare Technologies, Energy, Agricultural Sciences to SaaS and IT services.

In my personal life, I enjoy the outdoors and playing as much golf as my free time will allow. I am married, have a 4 year old daughter and live in the Atlanta area.
