About Me

My Name is Trey, I'm currently enrolled in the Georgia Tech Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp. I am a beginner in Web Development, but look forward to the challenge and hope that I can learn many of these new skills. Professionally, I have mostly been working in Management, Business Development and Sales. I have worked for both small and large companies and I also owned and operated my own company for about 10 years.

In the mid-90's, I obtained a B.S. in Industrial Management from Georgia Tech. I have found that background extremely useful in just about everything I have been involved in. Good Management principles and practices generally go un-noticed, yet poor Management permeates through all levels of a Project and Organization.

My experiences have touched many different fields and industries. I have been involved with Medical Devices and Technologies, Energy Technolgies, Water Purification, Agricultural Storage Containers, Golf Course Agronomy and Turf Sciences. In my personal life, I enjoy the outdoors and play as much golf as my free time will allow. I am married, have a 3 year old daughter and live in Cumming, Georgia.

My goal is to restructure my career into Sales, Marketing and Project Management for Full-Stack Web Services and Applications. Completing the MERN Stack Bootcamp has allowed me to quickly learn the technologies involved on a comprehensive level.
